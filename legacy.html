Legacy

HandyWorks is Chiropractic and Acupuncture Office Management Software written in Microsoft Access. It is also suitable for Physical Therapists, Podiatry or Medical offices. Office Management includes HCFA and ANSI 5010 electronic billing, office accounting, patient scheduling, visit tracking, doctor productivity, and much much more. Compatible SOAP Note Systems: ChiroWrite.
