About HandyWorks

Professional Chiropractic Practice Management

HandyWorks is a comprehensive practice management software designed specifically for chiropractic offices. Developed by Dr. Steven Schram, this software streamlines administrative tasks and enhances patient care.

Key Features

  • Patient Management: Complete patient records and history
  • Appointment Scheduling: Easy scheduling and calendar management
  • Billing & Insurance: Electronic billing and insurance processing
  • Electronic Health Records: Digital patient charts and notes
  • Reporting & Analytics: Comprehensive practice reports
  • Text Messaging: Integrated patient communication via ClickSend

System Requirements

  • Windows operating system
  • Microsoft Access database
  • Network capability for multi-user access
  • Internet connection for updates and support

Support & Updates

HandyWorks is regularly updated with new features and improvements. Support is available via phone and email for all registered users.
